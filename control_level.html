Controlling the level of audio effects

We can train AMSS-Net to perform more detailed AMSS such as “apply heavy lowpass to vocals” and “apply light lowpass to vocals.” Users can control the level of audio effects by simply injecting adverbs such as `light’ instead of a laborious search for an appropriate parameter configuration.
